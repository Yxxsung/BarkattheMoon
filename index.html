Soapy, Furry Fun!

Bark at the moon Grooming is a local greenwood dog groomer located off of Polk Street close to The Social of Greenwood and Big Peach Gym.
We offer a variety of services from washing, to hair cuts and more! we can accommodate any dog breed from small to ginormous in our private suite. Come enjoy our waiting area with free coffee and water while you wait for your furry friend to complete their beautiful makeover!
